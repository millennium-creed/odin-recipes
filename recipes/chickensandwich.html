How to Make a Chicken Sandwich

An amazing chicken sandwich with honey butter, spices, seasonings, and your choice of condiments and toppings.

On this webpage, I will teach you how to make an amazing spicy (or regular if you prefer) chicken sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Halal chicken breasts
  • Buttermilk
  • Your choices of seasonings, herbs, and spices
  • Honey butter
  • Pickle juice
  • Tapatio hot sauce

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts
  2. Let marinade overnight
  3. Get frying oil
  4. Let simmer in frying oil
  5. Make special dip
  6. Add toppings of choice
  7. Enjoy!
